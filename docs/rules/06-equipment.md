## Weapons

Every weapon has the following attributes:

- **Hands** — 1 or 2. Two-handed weapons are powerful but block shield use.
- **BN Modifier** — three sub-values:
  - *Melee* — standard close-range attacks
  - *Charge* — when making a Charge (full rules in the core book)
  - *Ranged* — thrown / missile use
  - A `—` means the weapon cannot be used for that attack type.
- **Damage Value** — dice rolled to determine inflicted damage. Maximum damage equals the maximum possible roll (e.g. longsword `1D10` capped at 10) — exceeded only by Critical Hits, the Warrior +1, or Techniques.
- **Critical Value** — when Critical Hits land. Lower values (sharp tips like rapiers) crit more often but tend to do less per normal hit and break on a Total Failure.

### Projectile weapons

Need a Ranged BN Modifier. Two extra attributes:

**Range** (effective range — beyond it, −1 BN and −1 Damage per liet over):

| Weapon | Range |
|---|---|
| Sling | 4 liets (~16 m) |
| Longbow | 5 liets (~20 m) |
| Greatbow / Crossbow | 10 liets (~40 m) |
| Arbalest | 15 liets (~60 m) |
| Other (e.g. thrown) | 3 liets (~12 m) |

Absolute maximum range is 2 × effective range. Weather (e.g. wind) can reduce effective range at WM discretion.

**Reload Time**:

| Weapon | Reload |
|---|---|
| Slingshot, short bow, longbow | 1 Segment |
| Crossbow | 5 Segments |
| Arbalest | 10 Segments (crank) |

## Armor

Three slots: **Body**, **Head**, **Shield**. One of each may be equipped.

- **Absorption** — reduces damage. Sum across all equipped pieces = **Total Absorption**. Warriors get +1 Total Absorption as a class benefit.
- **Armor Modifier** — affects movement; usually negative. Applied to Base IN, Base BN, Base DN.

If a character switches to a two-handed weapon, they lose the shield's Absorption.

## General goods

All characters begin with:
- **Backpack** — large cloth bag with shoulder straps.
- **Waterskin** — three days of water.
- **Hemp Rope** — 3 liets (~12 m) starting amount.
- **Torch** — provides light for ~1 hour.
- **Flint Set** — start fires.

## Money

The currency of Wares Blade is the **golda (G)**, a silver coin accepted across Ahan.

- **1 gold golda** = 100 silver golda
- **1 silver golda** = 10 copper golda

So 1 gold golda = 1,000 copper golda.
