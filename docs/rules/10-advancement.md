# 10 — Advancement

> Source: Wares Blade Playkit, Chapter 4 (p. 35)

## Proficiency Points (PP)

Whenever an Action Roll produces a Perfect Success or a Total Failure, the character earns 1–20 PP in the Skill used. Amount depends on impact:

| Impact | Perfect Success | Total Failure |
|---|---|---|
| Minimal | 5 | 1 |
| Non-Critical | 10 | 5 |
| Tremendous | 20 | 10 |

If LUC was used, multiply PP by the **total number of dice rolled**.

If no Skill was used, the WM picks an appropriate one. If the character doesn't have it, they gain it at Level 0 and only earn **half** PP. The Skill cannot be used in Action Rolls until raised to ≥ Level 1.

## Completion Bonus

When characters finish an adventure, they all receive a **Completion Bonus** based on objectives accomplished. WM guideline:

| Event | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Completed an objective set by the WM | +100 |
| Destroyed a Footman | +50 |
| Acquired a Footman | +100 |
| Destroyed a Courser | +100 |
| Acquired a Courser | +150 |
| Destroyed a Maledictor | +200 |
| Acquired a Maledictor | +250 |
| At least one character suffered Heavy Damage in battle | +30 |
| At least one character Incapacitated in battle | +50 |
| Previously acquired Ryude was damaged | +(Durability Loss × 3) |
| Acquired valuable treasure | +(Golda value / 500) |
| Player cooperated with WM (WM judgment) | +30 |
| Other (WM choice) | −50 to +50 |

## Spending PP and Completion Bonus

At the end of the adventure, players raise Skill Levels:

- **Cost to raise from current Level → next Level**: `(Current Level + 1) × 100` PP in that Skill.
- **Acquiring a Level 1 Skill from scratch**: 200 PP for Combat Skills, 150 PP for Adventure Skills.
- **Specialized Skills** require a dedicated instructor and long training — cannot be purchased this way.

If PP is insufficient, **Completion Bonus** can cover the shortfall.

## LUC Reserves

Any unspent Completion Bonus can be **banked as LUC Reserves**.

- 100 LUC Reserve points → 1 Available LUC at any time during play.
- This **cannot exceed the character's initial LUC Ability Score**.
